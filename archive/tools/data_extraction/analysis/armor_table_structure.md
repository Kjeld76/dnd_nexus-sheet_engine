## Columns identified:
1. **Rüstung**: Name
2. **Rüstungsklasse (RK)**: Formula or value
3. **Stärke**: Strength requirement (e.g., "STÄ 13")
4. **Heimlichkeit**: Stealth penalty ("Nachteil")
5. **Gewicht**: Weight in kg
6. **Kosten**: Cost in gold pieces (GM)

## Armor Proficiency Rules (PHB 2024)
- **Consequences of non-proficiency**:
    - Disadvantage on any W20 test involving Strength or Dexterity.
    - Inability to cast spells.
- **Shields**: Must be proficient to benefit from the AC bonus.
